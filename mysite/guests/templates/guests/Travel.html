Travel

Our wedding celebration takes place in Ko Olina, where there are several resort options. There are also some Airbnb options within the community and nearby. There are also options in Waikiki, which is about a 40 minute drive away without traffic.

The best way to get around Oahu is to rent a car. There are multiple rental car companies by the airport and in Waikiki. Oahu has Lyft and Uber here as well. Most of it is concentrated in Honolulu, so if you're visiting and are looking to do some sight seeing outside of Honolulu, we do recommend not relying on that as a primary way of travel. But they the best ways of getting to and from our venue if you're planning on drinking with us at our reception!
